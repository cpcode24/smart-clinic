## User Story  

### As an **admin**, you can:

1. Log into the portal with your username and password to manage the platform securely
2. Change password when needed.
3. Log out of the portal to protect system access
4. Add doctors to the portal
5. Edit doctor's profile
6. Delete doctor's profile from the portal
7. Run a stored procedure in MySQL CLI to get the number of appointments per month and track usage statistics

**Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Admin is able to securely log in and logout, and change password when needed.
   * **Priority:** High
   * **Story Points:** 3
2. Admin is able to add, edit and delete doctor's profiles seamlessly.
   * **Priority:** High
   * **Story Points:** 6
3. Admin is able to view number of appointments, doctors and patients in a dashboard.
   * **Priority:** Medium
   * **Story Points:** 2


### As a **patient**, you can:

1. View a list of doctors without logging in to explore options before registering
2. Sign up using your email and password to book appointments
3. Log into the portal to manage your bookings
4. Log out of the portal to secure your account
5. Log in and book an hour-long appointment to consult with a doctor
6. View my upcoming appointments so that I can prepare accordingly
7. View doctor's feedback and test results

**Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Patient can view the list of doctors and search for doctors using criteria such as zip code, city, etc.. without loging in.
   * **Priority:** High
   * **Story Points:** 4
2. Patient is able to sign up using email and password, and verify email address before being able to securely log in and logout.
   * **Priority:** High
   * **Story Points:** 3
3. Patient can book, edit or cancel an hour long consultation with a doctor.
   * **Priority:** Medium
   * **Story Points:** 6
4. Patient can view upcoming appointments with a doctor.
   * **Priority:** Medium
   * **Story:** 2
5. Patient can view doctor's feedback and test results.
   * **Priority:** Low
   * **Story:** 2

### As a **doctor**, you can:

1. Log into the portal to manage your appointments
2. Log out of the portal to protect my data
3. View my appointment calendar to stay organized
4. Mark your unavailability to inform patients only the available slots
5. Update your profile with specialization and contact information so that patients have up-to-date information
6. View the patient details for upcoming appointments so that I can be prepared

**Acceptance Criteria:**
1. A doctor can securely log in and log out.
   * **Priority:** High
   * **Story Points:** 2
2. Doctor can view and manage appointments with patients in the appointment calendar.
   * **Priority:** High
   * **Story Points:** 3
3. Doctor can set availability times, and update profile information.
   * **Priority:** High
   * **Story Points:** 6
4. Doctor can view upcoming appointments with a patient.
   * **Priority:** Medium
   * **Story:** 2
